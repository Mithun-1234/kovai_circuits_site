About Us

A focused team turning emerging technology into job-ready skill

Domains Covered 04

Training Since 2018

Kovai Circuits is a technology training and R&D company dedicated to building real, applied capability in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, the Internet of Things and Electric Vehicle technology. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and the hardware-software systems industry actually runs on.

Our trainers are practising engineers. Every program is mapped to live lab work, embedded design, model building, simulation and prototyping, so learners leave with proof of skill, not just a certificate.

Alongside training, our in-house lab delivers IoT research, prototyping and custom development for partners scaling industrial and academic projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which technologies do you provide training in?

Our core domains are Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Internet of Things (IoT) and Electric Vehicle (EV) technology, along with embedded systems and applied machine learning. Programs run for institutions, enterprises and individual professionals.

Do you offer on-campus bootcamps for colleges?

Yes. Our Institutional Bootcamps are accelerated, certification-based programs delivered on campus with hands-on lab kits and an industry-aligned curriculum, built around practical embedded design, EV simulation and machine learning.

Can training be customised for our company's tech stack?

Absolutely. Corporate Enterprise Modules are tailored to your team's stack, whether that's advanced automation, edge analytics or heavy industrial IoT, and can be delivered on-site or remotely.

Do you take on development and R&D projects?

Yes. Beyond training, our in-house lab delivers IoT research, hardware prototyping, embedded firmware and custom software development for partners scaling industrial and academic projects.

Will learners receive a certificate?

Every completed program includes a Kovai Circuits certification. More importantly, learners finish with project-based, portfolio-ready deliverables that demonstrate real, applied skill.
